Welcome to Animals Around Us

Mammals

Mammals are warm-blooded animals that have fur or hair and typically give birth to live young.

  • Dogs: Known for their loyalty and companionship.
  • Cats: Independent and graceful creatures.
  • Elephants: The largest land mammals with remarkable intelligence.

Birds

Birds are feathered creatures that lay eggs and have wings for flying.

  • Eagles: Majestic birds of prey with keen eyesight.
  • Parrots: Colorful birds known for their ability to mimic human speech.
  • Penguins: Flightless birds adapted for swimming in cold waters.

Reptiles

Reptiles are cold-blooded animals covered in scales or bony plates.

  • Snakes: Slithering creatures with fascinating patterns and venomous species.
  • Turtles: Ancient reptiles known for their protective shells.
  • Crocodiles: Large, predatory reptiles found in tropical habitats.

Marine Life

Marine animals live in water and include a wide variety of species.

  • Dolphins: Intelligent marine mammals known for their playful behavior.
  • Sharks: Apex predators with sharp teeth and a streamlined body for swimming.
  • Jellyfish: Beautiful yet delicate creatures found in oceans worldwide.
